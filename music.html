About My Band

I got a band called "soaring majesty". We formed in 2023 with 4 instrumental member and 1 vocalist. I play bass in my band. Our band has performed a lot of show in UITM such as Merbok Got Talent, Merbok Health Festival and Projek 90an. All of our bandmates are mostly from Alor Setar.

My Music Journey

My journey into the world of music has been nothing short of magical. From learning my first instrument to performing on stage, music has always been my passion.I started learning guitar and keyboard at the age of 15. I will always have dream to start a band back then now ive got it. I cherish the moments I share with my bandmates, creating something truly unique.

My Favorite Band

Their lead singer, Kurt Cobain has always been a special place in my heart.
